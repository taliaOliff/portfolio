{⬇ about me ⬇}

Hi! My name is Talia Oliff! I'm a late 2003 baby born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio, along with my younger brother and sister, twins, born in early 2007. I attend Miami University as a games & simulation major with minors in computer science and creative writing. I love my classes and am very passionate about exploring all of their applications. I love to create, whether that be through code, writing, or visual art!

I hope to create games and apps to help people, whether through escape, learning, and/or enjoyment. I've been honing my craft my whole life, from writing stories for my siblings when they were learning to read to keeping my fourth grade daily pages all these years. This love eventually expanded into coding and creating games. I've made sight word games for my mom's kindergarten class, designed games for nearly every one of my birthday parties, and have consistently written since second grade.

In addition to games and apps, I'm working on a fantasy series as well as some other stories and novels! On top of academics, I participate in Hillel, a national Jewish organization at colleges, and am part of aerial silks. Other clubs I’ve participated in include astronomy club and the student disability club. I’m very passionate about invisible disabilities and accessibility and hope to showcase that priority with my future works.

In my free time, I work on my creative projects, spend time with friends and family, read, watch shows/videos, and/or try to get better at certain skills! Some interesting things about me include my love for swings, the five years I spent working in a preK classroom, and that I'm currently in the process of learning ASL!
