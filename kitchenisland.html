Whoopsie Daisies Kitchen Island Re-Treat Finish Lime

In my IMS 453 (Indie Game Development) class I spent the semester working on a project with a studio of five people. I was in charge of the puzzles, implementing the artists' animations into Unity, the player movement, and organizing some of our team meetings. This was my first time in a studio and I learned a lot from my peers about project management, scope, and working both independently and together on different facets of the same product. Kitchen Island Re-Treat follows a wandering food biologist trying to set their fantastical environment right again by traveling through and returning wildlife to their rightful habitats.
