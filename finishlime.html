Whoopsie Daisies Kitchen Island Re-Treat Finish Lime

In my CSE 251 (Intro to Game Programming) class I gained a lot more experience with Unity. I decided to try my hand at a racing game for my final project, where the character you're playing as is based around a lemon theme and you have to either destroy/avoid rock obstacles as you attempt to stay on the track for as long as possible. You can move between the three lanes through the right and left arrow keys while the up arrow is used to shoot the rocks.
