Whoopsie Daisies Kitchen Island Re-Treat Finish Lime

In my CSE 251 (Intro to Game Programming) class I gained a lot more experience with Unity. For my final project, I developed and created the art for an iteration of a game I hope to further explore through a 3D version in Unreal for an independent study. But for Finish Lime here in its early stages, you play as a lemon-themed racer trying to avoid and/or destory the pink swirly rocks, catch the power-ups as they fall, and last as long as you can on the track. You can move between the three lanes through the right and left arrow keys and shoot at the rocks through the up arrow key. The longer you stay on the track and the more rocks you shoot, the higher your score. Click the play button below to see how high of a score you can get!
