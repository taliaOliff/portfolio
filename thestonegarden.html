In early 2020 I submitted to a short story contest. I may not have won but I was fortunate to recieve the following from one of the organizers and will forever be grateful:


"Hello again, I am sorry to send you another email about the Short Story Contest, but I wanted to let you know that you were one of the top 10 finalists in your category. You may not have officially placed, but being in the top 10 is a major accomplishment, especially when there were 92 other competitors. I thought I would share this in order to encourage you. I know that putting your work out there can be difficult and I want to applaud your effort. On a personal note, I loved this story! I love fantasy and adventure and this story had both. I also enjoyed how the ending had a cliffhanger. Who stole the ancient book and why? I could see this character in a Young Adult novel. I hope you will consider entering the contest again next year. Thanks again for sharing your story, Ashley L. Short Story Contest Chair Adult Services Specialist Williamsburg Branch Library"
